BUT WHY?

Stopping wars and promoting world peace is essential for the well-being and survival of humanity. It is a problem that has gone on long enough already, driven by the need for power and resources.

Wars result in the loss of countless innocent lives, displacement of people, and long-term psychological trauma. They also cause significant economic losses, often resulting in environmental degradation, pollution, and destruction of natural resources.

Wars can have long-term consequences for generations to come, perpetuating cycles of violence and conflict. By working together towards these goals, we can create a more peaceful and harmonious world for all.
